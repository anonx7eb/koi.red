blumepopo - apocalypsis (2020)

Blume popo is a five member indie rock band with shoegaze and math rock influences. In October of 2019 I came across their music video for Veronica; a mesmerizing mix of gazey guitars, passionate vocals, and contemporary dance. I must've watched it five times before coming back to reality. Apocalypsis follows in the same vein as their last EP umi to dokuyaku, but it doesn't quite have a Veronica of its own. Miko's vocals have tamed some but are still powerful in her signature, shouty, R-rolling way. They're one of the most exciting underground acts for me right now and I'd love to see a full-length from them some day.

7/10
