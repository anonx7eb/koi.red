月の丘

呼ばれた人は　たやすく登れてしまう
月の丘　あの子はまだ
わたしたち　幾つも約束をしたまま

嵐の夜に吹く風　頬は濡れて
涙雨　あの子のeyes
永遠に逆らいながら
生きてきたの

空をさいて　輝く影
星に誓い　消えてゆく　微笑みは
丸い月になって　丘に潜んだの

A Hill of the Moon

Those who are called upon could climb with ease
The hill of the moon, and the child who is yet to be called upon
Our handful of promises remain

The wind on stormy nights soaks the cheeks
Tear of rain, and the child’s eyes
Rebellious without an end
As the child lived to this day

The sky shattered by a lucent shadow
The smile that faded away as it made an oath to the stars
Has turned into a full moon, embedded within the hill
