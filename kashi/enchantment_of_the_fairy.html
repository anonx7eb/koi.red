Enchantment of the Fairy

From the 23℃ air conditioner
Lots of fairies are marching out
Somewhere within their plumages lie the trickling sweat drops
Lightly rolling and tinkling

From the silently bending ceiling comes plenty of fruit
Falling upon and sinking into my forehead
Sharing the rest of our dreams; blurring the sceneries
We shall meet once more, my illusion

With tenacity against any magic, the glitter persists
