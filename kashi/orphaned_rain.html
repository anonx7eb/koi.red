みなしごの雨

星屑のさざ波が
孤児たちを濡らしてく
雪が夜に積もるのと
涙が瞳に落ちるのと
同じ音色がこだまして

人々の祭壇に
テトラポッドの雨が降る
祈る掌に砕け
見上げた空に舞い上がる
ひかり　七色の銃弾

窓の外には　もう逢えぬ人
下を向けば　渡り鳥の群れ

Orphaned Rain

The ripples of stardust
Left the orphans soaking wet
The piling up of snow at night and
The tear falling into one’s eyes
Echoes in the same tonal color

To each of their own altars
The rain of tetrapod pours and
Crumbles upon their praying palms
Then soars into the skies above
Light, bullets of seven colors

Outside the window are people who can no longer meet
If you look below, a flock of bird of passage is present
