Dedicated to the translation of Ichiko Aoba's work.

Firstly, we don't believe that translations of Aoba's work should be made.

It is not something that anyone has the right to alter in her place, even if they can.

However, we recognise that translations will invariably be made over time.

Therefore, we exist to make sure that her every word and intention is treated with the utmost reverance.
