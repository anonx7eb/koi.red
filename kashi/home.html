Dedicated to the translation of Ichiko Aoba's work.

Firstly, I don't don't believe that translations of Aoba's work should be made. There is an incredible amount of poetry lost in conversion.

Her work is not something that anyone has the right to alter in her place, even if they can.

Regardless, translations will be made over time.

Therefore, this page exists to ensure that Ichiko's every word and intention is treated with the utmost reverance.

Please don't paste these translations anywhere as they are highly subject to change. Please link to this main page instead.
